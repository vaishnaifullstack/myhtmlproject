ARJIT SINGH

Arijit Singh is an Indian playback singer and music composer.
The recipient of several accolades including a
National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards,
he has recorded songs in several Indian
languages, predominantly Hindi and Bengali.
Born: 25 April 1987 (age 36 years), Jiaganj Azimganj
Spouse: Koel Roy (m. 2014)
Parents: Kakkar Singh, Aditi Singh
